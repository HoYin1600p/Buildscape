## Overview

Welcome to **BuildScape** - the ultimate expansion for builders, architects, and creators! This mod adds a massive collection of new building blocks designed to spark creativity and transform your Minecraft world into a true masterpiece. From sleek modern tiles and rustic wood planks to glowing crystals, smooth marbles, and industrial metals, BuildScape gives you every texture and color you could imagine.

Whether you're crafting a cozy village, a grand castle, or a futuristic city, these blocks fit perfectly into any theme or building style. Each block has been carefully designed to blend seamlessly with vanilla Minecraft while adding fresh, unique flair to your builds.

## Block Families

### Colored Sand

8 colored sand blocks in black, blue, green, orange, pink, red, white, and yellow. They fall like regular sand, so be careful where you place them!

Each color also has a full sandstone set - regular sandstone, smooth sandstone, plus slabs, stairs, and walls. That's 40 sandstone blocks total across all colors.

### Colored Tiles

Beautiful decorative tiles in all 16 Minecraft dye colors. Each color comes as a full block, slab, stairs, and wall - 64 blocks total. Perfect for modern builds, bathrooms, kitchens, or anywhere you want that polished tile look.

### Concrete Variants

All 16 colored concrete blocks now have slabs, stairs, and walls. That's 48 new concrete building blocks to work with.

### Pillars

Four pillar types: Quartz, Stone, Deepslate, and Mossy. These aren't just decorative - they're interactive!

Pillars automatically connect when you stack them vertically, forming seamless columns. Right-click with any item to display it on top of the pillar. You can also add up to 5 dye colors to create custom particle effects with patterns like beams, spirals, fountains, pulses, rings, bursts, and snowflakes. Each pillar gets its own unique ID, so you can configure them individually if you want.

### Layers

Three types of stackable layers that work like snow layers (1-8 layers high):

- **Wool Layers** - All 16 dye colors. Great for adding texture and color to builds.
- **Leaf Layers** - 10 types covering all vanilla trees plus azalea and mangrove variants. Can be waterlogged too.
- **Moss Layers** - Natural green moss layers for that overgrown look.

### Hedges

10 hedge types for all the vanilla trees plus azalea and mangrove. These automatically connect to each other, creating seamless garden walls. Players can walk through them without taking damage, and they have custom footstep sounds. Hedges can be low (1 block) or tall (2 blocks) depending on how they connect.

### Chains

Decorative chains in various materials. Small chains come in copper variants (normal, exposed, weathered, oxidized), gold, diamond, emerald, netherite, and ancient steel. Large chains are available in iron, gold, diamond, emerald, netherite, and ancient steel. All chains are climbable just like vanilla chains.

### Copper Variants

Complete copper building sets for all four oxidation states. Each state has 11 variants: regular blocks, cut copper, chiseled copper, copper bulbs, copper grates, plus slabs, stairs, and walls. That's 44 copper blocks total. Copper oxidizes over time just like vanilla, and you can wax it to prevent oxidation.

### Glass Variants

**Glazed Glass** - Frosted glass in all 16 colors, available as blocks and panes. Made by smelting stained glass.

**Mosaic Glass** - Decorative mosaic pattern glass in all 16 colors, also as blocks and panes. Can be crafted from glazed glass or stained glass.

### String Lights

16 colored string lights that you can customize. Right-click with dyes to change the string color, and right-click again to toggle the light on or off. They can face any direction and emit light when lit. Perfect for cozy outdoor areas or party decorations.

### Big Candles

18 large candle variants - all 16 standard colors plus amethyst and sculk variants. Light them up with flint and steel or a fire charge.

### Decorated Pots

17 decorated pot variants in all 16 colors plus the base pot. Break them to reveal pottery sherds just like vanilla.

### Rose Vines

Four colors of climbing rose vines: red, blue, black, and white. They grow and spread like vanilla vines, perfect for romantic builds or garden decorations.

### Icicles

Three icicle types: single hanging icicles, icicle blocks, and packed icicle blocks. They melt in warm biomes just like ice, so use them in cold builds or winter-themed areas.

### Overlays

Two overlay types: moss and snow. These are super thin decorative layers (just 1 pixel thick) that sit on top of other blocks. Players can walk through them, they don't cause suffocation, and they don't block vision - perfect for adding texture without changing your build's structure.

### Wood Variants

**Ashpen Wood** - Complete wood set with planks, slabs, stairs, fences, fence gates, buttons, and pressure plates.

**Bamboo Variants** - Expanded bamboo set with blocks, stripped blocks, slabs, stairs, fences, fence gates, signs, and boats.

**Mangrove Variants** - Logs, leaf layers, hedges, boats, and signs.

**Wood Walls** - Wall variants for various wood types like dark oak and jungle.

## Crafting

Most blocks follow standard Minecraft crafting patterns. Slabs are 3 blocks in a row, stairs use the L-shape pattern, and walls are 6 blocks in 2 rows. You can also use the stonecutter for most conversions, which is more efficient.

**Special Recipes:**
- 4 colored sand = 1 sandstone block
- Smelt sandstone to get smooth sandstone
- Smelt stained glass to get glazed glass
- Craft glazed glass to get mosaic glass
- 9 copper nuggets = 1 copper ingot (and vice versa)

## Installation

1. Install Minecraft Forge 40.3.11 or higher for Minecraft 1.18.2
2. Download the BuildScape mod file
3. Place it in your `mods` folder
4. Launch Minecraft and start building!

---

## Compatibility

BuildScape works with Minecraft 1.18.2 and requires Forge 40.3.11 or higher. All blocks are properly tagged for compatibility with other mods, and the mod works great with JEI for recipe viewing.
